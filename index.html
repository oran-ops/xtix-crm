🛡 Objection Handling
Every objection = a question in disguise (Salesforce BP #16). Don't argue — uncover the real pain behind it.
"Happy with our Platform"
What's hidden: Doesn't see value in switching
Optimal response:
"What do you like most about it? If XTIX keeps that + adds data ownership — worth 15 minutes?"
Principle: Agree first, then add value
"No time right now"
What's hidden: Doesn't see ROI
Optimal response:
"If I show you how much you're overpaying — worth 15 minutes?"
Principle: Prove quick ROI, not features
"Too expensive / Not in budget"
What's hidden: Hasn't connected to savings
Optimal response:
"XTIX is 2.5% — what are you paying now? The difference pays for itself. Shall we calculate together?"
Principle: Turn the ROI into a simple calculation
"Don't know XTIX"
What's hidden: Lacks social proof
Optimal response:
"Would [known client] be enough? They switched to us 6 months ago."
Principle: Social proof with a specific name
"Need to ask my partner"
What's hidden: Not the decision maker
Optimal response:
"What do they need to know? I'll prepare a specific summary."
Principle: Help them sell internally
"We'll think about it"
What's hidden: Not enough urgency
Optimal response:
"What's making you hesitate? Let's solve it right now."
Principle: Uncover the real blocker
